A one-liner is a sequence of commands that does something useful, interesting, in just one line!

A *good* one-liner is ...

  • Useful, practical for someone using bash, ksh family of shells.
  • Short, clear, easy to understand.
  • Something very interesting or cool.

If you know a *good* one-liner, please do not hesitate to post and share with everyone!

Do NOT post a one-liner if ...

  • ... it is too complicated and hard to read.
  • ... it is only marginally useful.
  • ... you don't fully understand how it works.
