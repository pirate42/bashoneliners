MISSION
Create a collection of practical or just pure awesome(!) bash or ksh one-liners or tips, with easy to use user interfaces for browsing, searching and contributing one-liners.
OPPORTUNITIES
Knowledge exchange between bash/ksh gurus, fresh ideas by new contributors, education of users new to Linux, coffee break entertainment.
AREAS OF COLLABORATION
Interface design, development, documentation, translations, testing.
SKILLS REQUIRED
Programming (web, mobile), web design, testing, translations, karma.
